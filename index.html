Expert Guide

Maximize Your Results

Proven strategies to optimize your nutrition and training.

01

Calculate Your TDEE

Total Daily Energy Expenditure is your baseline for nutrition planning.

  • BMR x Activity Multiplier
  • Sedentary: BMR x 1.2
  • Moderate: BMR x 1.55
  • Athlete: BMR x 1.725
02

High-Calorie Dense Foods

Pack more calories per serving with these powerhouses:

  • Nuts & Nut Butters (600+ kcal/100g)
  • Olive Oil & Ghee (120 kcal/tbsp)
  • Avocados (160 kcal/100g)
  • Whole Milk & Cream
  • Dried Fruits & Granola
03

Protein Timing Strategy

Distribute protein across 4-6 meals for optimal absorption:

  • Breakfast: 30-40g protein
  • Pre-workout: 20-30g
  • Post-workout: 40-50g (within 30 min)
  • Before bed: Casein or paneer
04

Meal Prep Like a Pro

Batch cook on Sundays to stay consistent all week:

  • Cook rice & rotis in bulk
  • Pre-portion protein sources
  • Prepare overnight oats
  • Store in glass containers
05

Smart Snacking

Bridge meal gaps with high-protein snacks:

  • Greek yogurt + honey + nuts
  • Protein shake with banana
  • Boiled eggs / chickpeas
  • Paneer tikka / chicken rolls
  • Mixed trail mix (200+ kcal)
06

Hydration & Recovery

Calories don't work without proper hydration and rest:

  • Drink 3-4 liters water daily
  • Sleep 7-9 hours for muscle recovery
  • Post-workout: banana + whey shake
  • Rest days are growth days
